> 
> The numbers alone are worth reading:
> 
> 💰 $3,850/week stipend (US) that's ~$61,600 for 4 months of full-time research
> 💻 ~$15k/month in compute funding
> 🧑‍🔬 Direct mentorship from researchers like Nicholas Carlini, Sam Bowman, Jascha Sohl-Dickstein
> 📄 80%+ of past fellows produced papers
> 🎯 25-50% received full-time Anthropic offers
> 
> Five workstreams this round:
> 
> → AI Safety (mechanistic interpretability, scalable oversight, model organisms)
> → AI Security (pentesting, vulnerability research, offensive security)
> → ML Systems & Performance (distributed systems, accelerator workloads)
> → Reinforcement Learning (RL environments, training data quality)
> → Economics & Societal Impacts (labor markets, human-AI collaboration)
> 
> Locations: London, Berkeley, or remote in US/UK/Canada.
> 
> Next cohort starts July 20, 2026.
> Deadline: April 26, 2026.
> 
> What makes this different from most fellowships, you work on empirical projects using open-source models and public APIs, not just internal tooling. The output is public research.
